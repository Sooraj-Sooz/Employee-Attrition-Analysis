# 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using **Power BI**.
The dashboard helps HR teams and business leaders analyze:

* Employee Attrition
* Workforce Demographics
* Department-wise Employee Distribution
* Salary Analysis
* Experience-based Attrition Trends

The goal of this project is to help organizations make **data-driven HR decisions** and improve employee retention.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

✔ Monitor employee attrition trends
✔ Identify departments with high attrition
✔ Analyze employee demographics
✔ Understand salary-related attrition
✔ Support HR decision-making with insights

# 📈 Dashboard Features

## 🔹 Attrition Trend by Experience

* Shows how employee attrition changes with experience.
* Employees with lower experience show higher attrition.

---

## 🔹 Attrition by Department

* Displays departments with the highest employee turnover.
* Administration and Sales departments have higher attrition.

---

## 🔹 Attrition by Gender

* Compares attrition trends between male and female employees.

---

## 🔹 Salary Slab Analysis

* Analyzes attrition based on salary ranges.
* Lower salary groups show higher attrition.

---

## 🔹 Employee Distribution

* Department-wise employee count
* Age-group employee analysis

# 📌 Key Insights

## 🔍 Attrition Insights

* Employees with low experience are more likely to leave.
* Administration department has the highest attrition.
* Male employee attrition is higher than female attrition.
* Employees with lower salaries show higher turnover.

---

## 👥 Workforce Insights

* Majority of employees belong to the 26–35 age group.
* Operations department has the highest employee count.
* Finance department has the lowest employee count.
